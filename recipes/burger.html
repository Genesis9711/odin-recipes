Description

Burger is a classic fast food consisting of a cooked patty placed inside a sliced bun. It's layered with lettuce, tomato, cheese, sauces, and optional toppings.

You can use beef, chicken, or plant-based patties. Toast the buns and load up with your favorite condiments for maximum flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 burger bun, sliced
  • 1 burger patty (chicken, veg, or beef)
  • 1 slice of cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 tablespoon mayo or ketchup

Steps

  1. Grill or pan-fry the patty until fully cooked.
  2. Toast the bun on both sides for a crispy texture.
  3. Spread sauces, add lettuce, cheese, and the patty inside the bun.
  4. Close and serve with fries or salad.
