Vada Pav

Home

Description

Vada Pav is Mumbai’s famous street food — a spicy potato fritter placed inside a bun (pav) and served with chutneys and fried green chili.

It’s often called the Indian burger and packs a punch with its garlic chutney and deep-fried vada. Best enjoyed hot and spicy.

Ingredients

  • 2 pav buns
  • 1 medium potato, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 2 tablespoons gram flour (for batter)
  • Green chutney and tamarind chutney

Steps

  1. Prepare mashed potato filling with spices and tempering.
  2. Shape into balls and dip in gram flour batter.
  3. Deep-fry until golden brown.
  4. Place inside pav with chutney. Serve with fried chili.
