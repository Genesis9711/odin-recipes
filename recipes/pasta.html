Description

Pasta is a staple Italian dish made from wheat flour and water. It's usually boiled and served with a variety of sauces like tomato, pesto, or cream-based.

You can enhance it with garlic, herbs, vegetables, or proteins like chicken and shrimp. Grate cheese over the top for added richness.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • Salt and herbs to taste

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Saut√© garlic in olive oil, add tomato puree and seasonings.
  3. Mix cooked pasta into the sauce and simmer for 2 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with grated cheese on top.
