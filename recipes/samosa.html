Description

Samosa is a popular Indian snack with a crisp outer layer and spicy potato-pea filling. Often deep-fried, it's enjoyed with tamarind or mint chutney and makes for a savory, satisfying treat.

Customize with different fillings like spiced lentils, paneer, or chicken. Best served hot, paired with tea or chutney. Great for evening snacks or party starters.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 3 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil to form a stiff dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.

  3. Divide dough, roll into discs, cut in half, and shape cones. Fill with mixture.
  4. Seal and deep-fry on medium heat until golden brown.
